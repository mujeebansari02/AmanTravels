Experience the Magic of Ajmer

Nestled in the heart of Rajasthan, Ajmer is a city where history, spirituality, and culture converge. From the sacred Dargah Sharif to the ancient Taragarh Fort, every corner tells a story of its rich heritage.

Top Attractions

Ajmer Sharif Dargah

The sacred shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, a revered Sufi saint, attracting millions of devotees.

Taragarh Fort

A magnificent hill fort offering panoramic views of the city and housing ancient water reservoirs.

A unique mosque built in just two and a half days, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.
